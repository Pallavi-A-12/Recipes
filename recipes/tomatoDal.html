Tomato Dal

INGREDIENTS

    For Pressure Cooking:

    • 1 cup Toor Dal (split pigeon peas), rinsed
    • 3-4 large Ripe Tomatoes, chopped
    • 1 medium Onion, diced
    • 2-3 Green Chillies, slit
    • ½ tsp Turmeric Powder
    • 1 tsp Red Chilli Powder
    • 1 tbsp Tamarind Paste (optional, for extra tang)
    • 3 cups Water
    • Salt to taste

    For Tempering (Tadka):

    • 2 tbsp Ghee or Oil
    • 1 tsp Mustard Seeds
    • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
    • 10 cloves Garlic, crushed or sliced
    • 2-3 Dried Red Chillies
    • 1 sprig Curry Leaves
    • A pinch of Hing (Asafoetida)

STEP BY STEP INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Cook the Dal: In a pressure cooker, add the rinsed toor dal, tomatoes, onions, green chillies, turmeric, red chilli powder, salt, and water.
  2. Pressure Cook: Secure the lid and cook for about 3-5 whistles on medium heat until the dal is completely soft and mushy. Let the pressure release naturally.
  3. Mash the Dal: Open the cooker and mash the dal slightly with a whisk or ladle to achieve a creamy consistency. Stir in the tamarind paste if using.
  4. Prepare the Tempering: Heat ghee in a small pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Add cumin seeds, dried red chillies, crushed garlic, curry leaves, and hing. Sauté until the garlic turns golden and aromatic.
  5. Combine: Pour this hot tempering over the cooked dal. Simmer for another 2-3 minutes to allow the flavours to meld.
  6. Garnish: Finish with freshly chopped coriander leaves.
